About Us

Overview

Ningshen Painting Work Private Limited is a professional house painting company located in the scenic Ukhrul district of Manipur, India. Our motto is "Service with Sacrifice", reflecting our commitment to providing top-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Our company's primary goal is to provide our customers with the best possible painting services at an affordable cost. We take pride in providing our customers with superior materials that are of the highest quality, and we offer great discounts to ensure that our customers are satisfied with their purchases.

Our products come with a warranty of up to 12 years, and our services come with a warranty of up to 5 years, ensuring that our customers receive top-notch services.

All of our painters are highly trained and certified by IGSSS and NABARD, ensuring that they possess the skills and expertise needed to complete any painting job to the highest standards. And our customers can rest assured that they are receiving the best possible service.
